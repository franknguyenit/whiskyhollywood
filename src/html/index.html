EB-5 BENEFITS

One investment offers one of the fastest path to permanent residency (Green Card) for the investor and entire immediate family (spouse and unmarried children under 21)
Green Card holders are eligible to travel and work anywhere in US
Investor’s children are eligible to attend U.S. universities and colleges at the same tuition cost as U.S. Residents and Citizens
The investor and entire immediate family are eligible to apply for U.S. citizenship
No language requirement
No education or business background requirement
Ability to qualify for financial aid and scholarship designated for Green Card holders and Citizens only

Benefits to investors

• Decision Making

IMM will have decision making control.

Typically, for other projects the Regional Center or the Fund Manager will decide without IMM having input, which may or may not be the best decision for the investors. IMM will be able to ensure that all decision making is in the best interest of the investors.

• Transparency

IMM will have full access to project documents and financials. This will allow to make sure documents are reviewed and reported annually by IMM.

• Communications

IMM will be the direct communicator with the Project and Investors. This will allow for more efficient responses to investors.
